How does it work?

Step 1: Learn Patterns

Science has shown that babies learn languages by recognizing patterns in the language used around them every day. The process also works well for adults.

Step 2: Use What's Learned

If you don't use a piece of knowledge often enough, you'll forget it - kind of like the mathematics you took in high school or college. heartlang reinforces learned patterns and vocabulary by making you use them.

Step 3: Put It All in Long-term Memory

After a while, you don't need to see a term as often. This process is part of putting what's learned into long-term memory, when you can recall it on demand.

Step 4: Rewards

A big encouragement for learning is getting rewards for doing well. We've got a badge and "hearts" point system that you can use to redeem cool stuff!
