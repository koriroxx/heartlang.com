Welcome to the one place to learn whatever language you want. With personalized scores, organized lessons, a resource center, mobile apps, and a community of other language learners and native speakers, you'll wonder why you never started learning a language sooner.

How does it work?

Step 1: Learn Patterns

Science has shown that babies learn languages by recognizing patterns in the language used around them every day. We believe process also works well for adults. We'll remind you to study, and show you the real language, not artificial textbook language.

Step 2: Use What's Learned

If you don't use a piece of knowledge often enough, you'll forget it - kind of like the mathematics you took in high school or college. heartlang reinforces learned patterns and vocabulary by making you use them, and reteaches you what you've forgotten. It's like flashcards, only cooler.

Step 3: Put It All in Long-term Memory

After a while, you don't need to see a term as often. This process is part of putting what's learned into long-term memory, when you can recall it on demand.

Step 4: Rewards

A big encouragement for learning is getting rewards for doing well. We've got a badge and "hearts" point system that you can use to redeem cool stuff!

For Free.

That's right, free. Although you'll see ads from time to time, you'll never have to pay to use our website. We believe that learning a language is something that anyone should be able to do, because it helps people connect with each other.
